Features

Everything in one place

From submitting a capital request to exporting a year-end audit report — LedgerPro handles the full financial lifecycle for student organizations.

Real-time Analytics

Dynamic charts powered by live data give officers and administrators immediate insight into categorical spending, revenue sources, and budget burn rate.

Permanent Audit Trail

Every dollar tracked with double-entry ledger integrity. All transactions are immutable and timestamped — full institutional compliance, always.

Capital Requests

Officers submit funding requests with full justification. Admins approve with one click — ledger entries happen automatically on approval.

Multi-Org Support

Manage dozens of student organizations from one institution dashboard, each with isolated ledger and its own permission structure.

PDF & Data Exports

Generate professional reports for any org, project, or time period — perfect for auditors, grant applications, and end-of-year reviews.

Role-based Access

Platform admins, institution superusers, and org officers each get exactly the access they need — nothing more, nothing less.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No complex setup. No IT department required. Your institution can go live today.

1

Institution Onboarding

Your admin sets up organizations, assigns superusers, and configures starting budgets through the LedgerPro platform panel.

2

Officers Get Access

Student officers receive secure logins scoped to their organization. View budgets, create projects, and submit requests immediately.

3

Request & Approve

Officers submit funding requests with purpose and amount. Admins approve or reject — double-entry accounting updates automatically.

4

Track & Report

Monitor spending with live dashboards, export PDFs for auditors, and analyze trends across all organizations in real time.
